About Me

My name is Nashariyah Binte Shamsudin, preferably addressed as Nash. I am currently 20 years old in my 2nd year of poly in Republic Polytechnic, studying in Digital Design and Development.

Raised with the artistic field, I started taking art classes in my free time and took N-Level Arts to broaden my knowledge and let my passion run free.

Despite being unable to enroll in an Art school/diploma, I still keep my passion alive by visiting museums and booths. After enrolling into D3D, I have hope that I am able to chase my dreams in arts but this time in the digital world.

Web Designer, Graphic Designer, Game Developer have started to pique my interest and I am willing to give my best to be accepted in these occupations.
